1. Working of details tag :
**When details tag is clicked, a open attribute gets added to it and its content (below summary tag) becomes visible.
**When details tag is again clicked, open attribute gets removed and its content hides.
**details summary{list-style:none OR display:inline-block} //to remove arrow marker of details tag
**details name="menu" : is used for details tag grouping i.e. if one detail tag is opened the other details tag will automatically closes
